Publications | Talks | Services

I am an associate professor at School of Information, Renmin University of China. Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University under supervision of Professor Jie Tang and Professor Juanzi Li. My research interest falls in area of data mining and natural language processing. My current research focuses on knowledge reasoning and knowledge grounded dialogue. More information about my experience and research can be found on Google Scholar.

I am looking for highly-motivated students to work with me. If interested, please drop me a message by email.
