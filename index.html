Short Bio

I am now a Ph.D. student (2nd year) at Knowledge Engineering Group in Department of Computer Science and Technology of Tsinghua University under supervision of Professor Jie Tang and Professor Juanzi Li.

I obtained my Bachelor degree in 2006 in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing JiaoTong University and Master degree in 2009 in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University. Then I worked in IBM CDL until 2012. My current researh interest falls in area of social network and heterogeneous information network analysis and modeling.

For more detailed personal information, please refer to my CV.

System

The core member of ArnetMiner, which is a free online service for academic social network analysis and mining. The system has collected information of over 1,000,000 researchers, 3,000,000 publication papers, and 8,000 conferences. The system is in operation on the internet since 2006 and has attracted nearly 4,320,000 independent IP accesses, with more than 300,000 page views per day.
